Rebecca Day | Allied Health Workforce Development Consultant

Rebecca Day is the Allied Health Workforce Development Consultant. Before moving to Sydney Local Health District in July 2021, Rebecca was a Project Officer at the Ministry of Health and has been an Allied Health Educator across two districts.

Rebecca is committed to developing the Allied Health Workforce. She has a passion to provide high quality education and training initiatives to support Allied Health Professionals and Allied Health Assistants with their delivery high quality person centred care. As the Allied Health Workforce Development Consultant, Rebecca supports the Aboriginal Allied Health Workforce through the NSW Health Allied Health Cadetship initiative. She advocates for the Allied Health Assistant Workforce to ensure they are best utilised across the district. Also included in her portfolio is facilitating the Allied Health Graduate Start Pathway, delivering clinical supervision training for the district, coordinating the District Allied Health Excellence Awards and providing bespoke initiative for the Allied Health Workforce.

As a life long learner, Rebecca holds a Master of Health Service Management, Graduate Certificate in Health Leadership and Management, Graduate Diploma in Adolescent Health And Welfare and a Bach of Applied Science - Leisure and Health (Diversional Therapy).

Mark Elkins | Research Education Consultant
